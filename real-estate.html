Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers to help you make informed decisions

How do I start buying a property with DreamHomes? +

Simply contact us through our website or call. Our agents will discuss your requirements, budget, and preferred locations. We then arrange property visits and guide you through the entire purchase process from selection to registration.

What documents are needed to buy a property? +

Essential documents include PAN card, Aadhaar card, address proof, income proof, bank statements (last 6 months), passport-size photos, and salary slips for home loan applications. Our team assists with all documentation requirements.

Do you charge any commission or hidden fees? +

We maintain complete transparency in our pricing. Our standard commission is 1-2% of the property value, discussed upfront. There are no hidden charges, and we provide a detailed breakdown of all costs before any transaction.

How long does the property buying process take? +

Typically, the process takes 30-60 days from selection to registration. This includes property selection, negotiation, loan approval (if required), legal verification, and final registration. We work to expedite this wherever possible.

Can you help with home loans and financing? +

Yes! We have partnerships with leading banks and financial institutions. Our financial advisors help you compare loan options, complete documentation, and secure the best interest rates suitable for your profile.

Do you offer property management services? +

Absolutely! We offer comprehensive property management for landlords including tenant screening, rent collection, maintenance coordination, legal compliance, and regular property inspections — all for a competitive monthly fee.

Are all your property listings verified? +

Yes, every property listed with us undergoes a thorough verification process including ownership verification, legal title checks, and physical inspections. This ensures you only deal with genuine and clear-titled properties.
