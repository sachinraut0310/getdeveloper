Help & info

Frequently asked

How long does shipping take? +

Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days within India. Express delivery (1-2 days) is available at checkout. International orders typically arrive within 7-12 days.

What's your return policy? +

We offer free returns within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unworn with original tags attached. Sale items can be exchanged but not refunded.

How do I find my size? +

Each product page has a detailed size guide with measurements. If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for a relaxed fit. Our fit team is available via chat to help.

Do you ship internationally? +

Yes, we ship to over 60 countries worldwide. Shipping rates and delivery times vary by location and are calculated at checkout.

Where are your products made? +

Our pieces are made in small ateliers across Italy, Portugal, and India. Every product page lists the country of origin and the workshop. Full transparency, always.

How do I care for my pieces? +

Care labels are sewn into every garment. We also include a care card with each order. Most natural fibers are best hand-washed cold or dry-cleaned to preserve their feel.

Do you offer gift wrapping? +

Yes — at checkout you can add complimentary gift wrapping with a handwritten note. Perfect for birthdays, anniversaries, or just because.
